ESP32 are as follows:

SX1278 MOSI to ESP32 GPIO23 (MOSI)
SX1278 MISO to ESP32 GPIO19 (MISO)
SX1278 SCK to ESP32 GPIO18 (SCK)
SX1278 CS to ESP32 GPIO5 (SS)
SX1278 RST to ESP32 GPIO14 (or any available GPIO)
SX1278 DIO0 to ESP32 GPIO2 (or any available GPIO)
SX1278 GND to ESP32 GND
SX1278 3.3V to ESP32 3.3V
